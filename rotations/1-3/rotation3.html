UV Printer

Our next 2 projects both involved the utilitzation of the UV Printer that CTIC has.

A UV Printer uses UV Lights to cure the paint onto the surface of the material. Therefore, the ink won't get off unless the material is scratched. Our VersaUV LEF-200 can print on any material, including: PVC, plastics, leather, fabric, canvas, metal, glass and wood.
