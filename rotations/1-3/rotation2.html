Stamp Modeling

In the second design challenge we had to make a ball roll the greatest distance possible but it had to finish within a defined area. To do this we used the materials given to make a long path that we were sustaining with our arms and we used the mats to try to guide the ball to the are that we had filled with things that stopped the movement of the ball before it left the defined area.

Our team won the challenge (again!) out of all the participants because we were really risky but were able to pull off what we had practiced at a long distance.
